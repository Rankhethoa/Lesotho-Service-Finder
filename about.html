Our Values

Community-first. Transparent. Inclusive. We believe local talent is our greatest resource and our platform exists to celebrate and amplify it.

Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We've got answers.

Is Service Finder free to use?
Yes! Browsing and searching for service providers is completely free for customers. Service providers can list their services and reach new clients at no cost.
How do I list my service on the platform?
Create an account via the My Account page. Fill in your service details, add a photo, set your pricing and location, and your listing will appear on the site for customers to find.
Which districts in Lesotho are covered?
Service Finder covers all 10 districts of Lesotho: Maseru, Berea, Leribe, Butha-Buthe, Mafeteng, Mohale's Hoek, Quthing, Qacha's Nek, Mokhotlong, and Thaba-Tseka.
How do I contact a service provider?
Click "View Service" on any listing to open the full profile. Each profile shows the provider's phone number so you can contact them directly to arrange your booking.
Are the service providers verified?
Providers self-register on the platform. We encourage customers to check provider details, ask for references, and use the view count and ratings as indicators of reputation. We are continuously working on formal verification features.
How is the "Top Service Providers" ranking determined?
The top providers ranking is based on profile view counts — providers whose pages are visited most often by customers are ranked highest. This helps surface the most popular and in-demand professionals on the platform.
